Instructions

The in-app version will be a concise list of instructions. There will be a link to a README which has more in-depth instructions and technical details.

Left click on a folder to go into it. Right click to go up a folder.

Left click on a file to add it to the queue of things you would like to box. It will show up in the menu to the right. You can then either left click on the file in the file tree, or left/right click it in the menu to the right to remove it from the queue.

Whenever you are done selecting your files, hit the "Box Selected" button at the bottom to open OpenLabeling, our boxing software. Hit "q" at any time to finish boxing, and the files will automatically be uploaded to Box.
